Good Ending

If the players choose to escape via the other
route, the player will enter a cave with a platform
held by chains above lava in the centre of the cave.
The monster is standing on that platform. Players
learn through a vision about a machine that turned the
entire world grey and filled with his minions terrorizing
locals. To restore the world, players must defeat the
monster. The monster’s death reverses the machine’s effect.
Players then return back to the daycare where they meet with
Sarah, who shares her experience during the time she had
disappeared. As she turns around to lead players inside the
daycare once again... something’s not quite right with her...
