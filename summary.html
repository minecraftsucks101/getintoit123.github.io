Summary of Daycare 2

Roblox Daycare 2

Roblox Daycare 2 is the sequel to Daycare 1. The game was created by Parwaz Studios on 19-3-2020. The story is a horror game where you try to survive the deadly monster and his loyal minions.
In the game, you play as a toddler/child in a daycare.
The game has different endings, good, bad, secret and more endings.
